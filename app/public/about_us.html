about pichaprint

from idea to prototype.

pichaprint turns a plain-english description into a complete design — a 3D-printable enclosure, a circuit diagram, firmware, and a bill of materials. no CAD, no EE degree, no friction.

join the waitlist try the generator

how it works

one prompt, start to finish.

idea

you have an idea or a project you want to build.

prompt

pitch-a-print generates your enclosure, circuit, firmware and instructions on how to assemble.

prototype

your idea is printed, the components are sourced and shipped to you to assemble.

what you get

one prompt. four ready-to-build pieces.

3D enclosure

an editable, multi-part casing you can orbit, explode and export to STL — ready for any printer.

circuit diagram

a clear block diagram of every component and how it wires together.

firmware

working, readable code generated for your board — flash it and go.

bill of materials

every part, quantity and price — so you know exactly what to buy.

our mission

our goal is simple: get anyone into hardware prototyping. whether you're a student, artist, entrepreneur, or curious tinkerer, you should be able to bring your ideas to life without friction.
